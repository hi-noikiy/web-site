Overview
Matrix DeFI Index (MDI) is a capitalized weighted index fund composed of the best DeFi protocols in the crypto market (including UNI, SUSHI, AAVE, SNX, MKR, COMP, YFI, CRV, GRT, etc.), which can track the performance of the DeFi asset in the overall market.
MDI adopts a market capitalization ratio and issuance method supported by real assets, which means that each MDI share is supported by the underlying crypto assets and can be purchased and withdrawed at any time.
Method
When users build MDI, the smart contract will purchase basic assets from Uniswap or other decentralized exchange, using the tokens in users' wallet to exchange with our asset. Through this way, MDI token has full support of reserve funds. With only one-click to withdraw at any time.
The index is constructed using Balancer smart pool, the pool keeps balancing and responding to the market change at real time.
The pool has no withdraw fees, but 0.7% transaction fee will be charged and rewarded to MDF holders.
Users are encouraged to deposite MDI into the liquidity pool or participate in staking to get the MDI reward in return
